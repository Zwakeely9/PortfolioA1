Restaurant Branding Package

This project was a branding package we had to create for a restaurant of our choosing. We could also create a restaurant ourselves which is what I did.
I created a branding package for a high-end vegan restaurant called Clean. I used Adobe Illustrator to bring my ideas to life, and created a final deliverable
that consisted of a logo, business card, envelope, and letterhead. I also created a website for the restaurant using Adobe XD.
