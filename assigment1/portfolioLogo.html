Trifecta Logo Case Study

This project was done for a personal client who has their own content creation brand. They asked for a logo and branding for their business which was called Trifecta Media. They wanted the logo to reflect their brand values and what their business ethics are.
I was able to reflect the client's requests in the logo and branding identity. Using Illustrator to construct the logo, I used a variety of triangular shapes and various shades of blue to
represent the client's brand values of honestly, integrity, and a commitment to accessible design for all.
